Welcome to Galaxy Tetris!

How to Play:

Tetris is a classic puzzle game where the goal is to stack falling blocks (tetrominoes) to create complete rows without any gaps. When a row is completed, it disappears, and the player earns points. The game ends when the stack of blocks reaches the top of the playing field.

Controls:

  • Left Arrow: Move the falling block left
  • Right Arrow: Move the falling block right
  • Down Arrow: Accelerate the falling block's descent
  • Up Arrow: Rotate the falling block
